Metal Tasting Spoons

This is a list of ice cream shops that use metal tasting spoons (MTS). Having MTS is not a requirement of a good ice cream shop. It is an indicator. There are plenty of great ice cream shops that do not use MTS. Use of MTS is merely an observation that several ice cream shops of a certain environment have this one thing in common. Without further ado, here is the list:

  • Sweet Rose Creamery
  • Salt & Straw
  • Odd Fellows
  • Red Wagon Creamery
